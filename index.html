About The NUE Green EXPO

What is NUE Eco-Smart City Expo?

As of right now in 2022, the eco-industry market cap is at $13 billion and this number is projected to grow to $51.09 billion by 2029. This means the green and sustainability market is an incredibly fast growing market and that means there is every opportunity for the BIPOC communities to leverage this growth to unlock economic and community growth. The goal of is to introduce and facilitate the creation of BIPOC eco-workforce and ecopreneurs with 6-figure eco-business plans and blueprints so we can carve out our own eco-niche and have a seat at the table in this fast growing industry.
